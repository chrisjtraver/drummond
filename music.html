Music

Zoom Out

Zoom out is a single released in May of 2016. This song featured Jake Howsam Lowe (guitarist with The Helix Nebula and Plini). The song reminds the listener to think of the bigger picture, and to remember they are a small part of it.

Getting Comfortable

Getting comfortable is Drummond’s first released from October 2015. This album features two Guest solos, one from guitarist Alex Frondelli on ‘Getting Comfortable’ and one from guitarist Sithu Aye in ‘ecotone’. This album is about coming into your own and enjoying who you are.
